**Design Movement:** Brutalism with Refined Edges

**Core Principles:**
1. Raw, honest presentation of information without embellishment
2. Bold typography as the primary visual element
3. High contrast and stark color blocks
4. Intentional "roughness" that signals authenticity

**Color Philosophy:**
Black (#000000) and white (#FFFFFF) foundation with a single accent: warning orange (#FF6B00). This palette communicates seriousness and urgency—the orange highlights critical decision points and calls-to-action. The starkness reflects the high-stakes nature of product strategy.

**Layout Paradigm:**
Full-bleed sections with hard edges. No rounded corners. Content arranged in dense, newspaper-style columns that break at unexpected points. Tables and charts interrupt text flow like editorial callouts.

**Signature Elements:**
1. Oversized section numbers (200px+) as background elements
2. Thick border separators (8-12px) between major sections
3. Monochrome data visualizations with single-color highlights

**Interaction Philosophy:**
Interactions are direct and immediate—no easing curves, instant state changes. Hover effects are binary (on/off). Clicking feels tactile and decisive, reinforcing the product manager's role as decision-maker.

**Animation:**
Minimal animation—only functional transitions. Section changes snap into place. Data reveals are instant, not gradual. The only smooth animation is scroll progress indicator.

**Typography System:**
All text: IBM Plex Mono (monospace for everything)
Headings: 700 weight, all caps, tight letter-spacing
Body: 400 weight, generous line-height for readability
Emphasis: 600 weight + orange color
